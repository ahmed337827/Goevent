OUR STORY

From Dream to Reality

Goevent was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make extraordinary travel experiences accessible to everyone. What started as a small family-owned agency in 2008 has grown into one of the most trusted names in the travel industry.

Our founder, Sarah Johnson, began this journey with nothing but a passion for travel and a dream to help others discover the world's wonders. Today, our team of 50+ travel enthusiasts continues her legacy, crafting unforgettable journeys for travelers worldwide.
